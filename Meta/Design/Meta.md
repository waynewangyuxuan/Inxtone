## Design Philosophy

**"Ink meets silicon."**

Merge traditional East Asian calligraphy aesthetics with modern CLI/developer tool sensibilities. Dark, sophisticated interface that feels both literary and technical.

**Principles:**
1. **Local-First Aesthetic** — Dark backgrounds, terminal-inspired, ownership feel
2. **Ink as Interaction** — Subtle ink splatter effects on user actions
3. **Minimal but Expressive** — High contrast, ample white space
4. **Typography-Forward** — Clean sans-serif for UI, monospace for code
5. **Progressive Disclosure** — Reveal complexity through animation and scroll
